## 1. Discovery Context

The eCommerce education and review ecosystem is mature but structurally flawed.

Despite an abundance of content, most merchants:
- still make poor early decisions
- overpay for tools
- scale prematurely
- confuse activity with progress
- fail to reach sustainable profitability

The market does not suffer from a lack of information — it suffers from a lack of **decision clarity**.

---

## 2. Core Problem

### Surface Problem
eCommerce founders cannot confidently decide:
- which platform to use
- which tools are necessary
- when to scale
- what to stop doing
- how to avoid irreversible mistakes

### Root Problem
Most available content:
- optimizes for clicks, not outcomes
- is biased by affiliate incentives
- focuses on tactics instead of systems
- ignores execution constraints
- decays quickly as tools and trends change

There is no independent, evergreen authority that explains **how eCommerce actually works as a system**.
